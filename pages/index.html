PARP Inhibitor Maintenance Therapy for Patients with Ovarian Cancer - a Shared Decision Tool
This tool will walk you through many of the common questions to consider when talking to your doctor about taking a PARP inhibitor after you finish chemotherapy. It is intended for patients who are completing chemotherapy for the first time for their ovarian/fallopian tube/primary peritoneal cancer.
